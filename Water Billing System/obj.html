Project Objectives

Automated Billing Process

To develop a system that automates the billing process, reducing manual errors and increasing efficiency in the calculation of bill amounts based on water consumption.

Online Payment Options

To provide customers with the convenience of online payment options, such as credit/debit cards and UPI, reducing the need for physical visits to pay bills.

Improved Customer Experience

To offer easy access to customer account information and payment history, improving overall customer experience and satisfaction.

Reduced Administrative Workload

To reduce the administrative workload by automating the billing process, freeing up resources for other important tasks.
