Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I pay my water bill?

    You can pay your water bill online through our website or mobile app, or in person at one of our payment centers. We accept payment via UPI, credit card, debit card, net banking, Paytm, and cash on delivery.

  • What happens if I don't pay my water bill on time?

    If you don't pay your water bill on time, late fees may be added to your bill and your water service may be disrupted. It's important to pay your bill promptly to avoid any inconvenience.

  • How do I check my water bill balance?

    You can check your water bill balance online through our website or mobile app, or by calling our customer service center.

  • What should I do if I have a billing dispute?

    If you have a billing dispute, please contact our customer service center. Our representative will assist you in resolving the issue.
